My History

I was born in the woods of Minnesota. My father was alpha of the pack, and I was the last of many siblings. We had our own large territory in the north where I trained and honed my skills. When I grew up, I realized the pack was too big, so I split off on my own. I spent several months as a lone wolf, honing my survival skills. I learned to both make friends and defend myself when necessary. Now I am searching for a new pack where I can let my abilities shine.
